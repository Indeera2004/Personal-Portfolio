What Are The Benefits To Closing The Wage Gap?

Not only would closing the wage gap help women support their families, but it also would help cut poverty rates in half for working women. Furthermore, it would provide a beneficial boost to the economy. Equal pay would increase the wages paid into the economy by $513 billion which represents almost 3% of the United States' Gross Domestic Income (GDP) in 2016.


Our Future

Spreading awareness and actively engaging in problem-solving solutions can help stop this trend! It is important for younger generations to be informed of the wage gap so as they themselves enter the workforce, they can help stop the problem and prepare for their own futures as well.
